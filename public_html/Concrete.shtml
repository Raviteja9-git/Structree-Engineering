Concrete Structures

STE will develop structural concepts for all parts of the project and determine appropriate design criteria for the project. The structural engineering team familiarise themselves with the Client objectives and then start to develop the engineering design basis and principles. A Concept Design report will be provided to allow the client to review progress and approve the way forward for Detailed Design.


The goal is to produce Detailed Design/Tender Design documentation that will sufficiently describe the project requirements and allow the Client to carry out a competitive tender process for the structural engineering works of the project. Finally Construction Drawings / GFCs/Working drawings are published.


STE gives design and drawings for retrofitting to existing structure by analysing existing conditions and structure stability, gives proper strengthening process. We do foundation underpinning for existing structure foundations (Red brick foundations, CMU foundations and Concrete foundations).
