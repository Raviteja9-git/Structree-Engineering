Structural Steel Design and Detailing Services:

We design and detail Structural steel structures ranging from simple to very high complex structures. Our structural design and detailing teams work on any type of structural projects includes trusses, Tubes structures, Pipe racks, Equipment supports, Staircases, Lift supporting system, Connections, Misc. steel (Railing, cage ladders etc.).


Our Team is expertise in making connection design and detailing for steel structures, steel to Concrete and masonry etc. We understand the complexity in making Misc steel, deliver the critical complexity locations with GA drawings and models for better understanding to client or fabricators.
