Wood Structures Services

STE develops the wood structural design and drawings for Single Family units, Multi family units, commercial structures and office structures etc.


We use different softwares like Wayerhouser Forte, Enercalc, Tekla Teds and some spread sheets for designing rafters, flush beams, drop beams, Columns, headers, Jambs, stud walls,Braced walls, floor sheathing, roof sheathing, connectors etc to coamplify NDS.


We use Southern yellow pine, Hem fir, TJI, Microlam, Glulam, Timber stands etc., for Rafters, Joists and beams. Spruce pine fir, PSL for Columns and stud walls. Simson strong ties and USP connectors for joists to beams, rafter to wall, beam to beam, hold downs connectors etc.


We do modifications for existing structures, strengthening to existing members for additional load, strengthening roof for snow drift due to new addition at top etc. We gives design and drawings for new additions to existing structures.
